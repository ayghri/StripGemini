Gemini Chat to Markdown Converter

Convert your Google AI Studio chat into clean Markdown format directly in your browser.

All operations happen on your browser, no data is logged or saved.

How to Use:

  1. Download your AIStudio Chat (History -> Gdrive )
  2. Select one or more Gemini Chat files.
  3. Check "Exclude User Prompts" if you only want the AI's responses.
  4. Check "Include Thought Blocks" if you want to see the AI's CoT.
  5. Click "Convert to Markdown".
  6. Your browser will automatically download the converted .md files.
